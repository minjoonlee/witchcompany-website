About WITCH Company

Building the financial core of global entertainment through K-Content infrastructure

Our Mission

Building Global Entertainment Financial Infrastructure Based on K-Content

WITCH Company is pioneering the intersection of entertainment and finance, creating modular infrastructure that enables global K-Content creators and enterprises to monetize, distribute, and scale their intellectual property through blockchain technology and modern fintech solutions.

Company Overview

WITCH Company specializes in entertainment technology and fintech infrastructure, with a focus on K-Content monetization and global distribution.

Our comprehensive platform integrates Web3 payments, tokenization, metaverse experiences, and short-form content distribution to serve the next generation of entertainment businesses.

Through strategic partnerships with industry leaders like Hyundai Motors, LG U+, Lotte Entertainment, and SM Entertainment, we have established ourselves as the trusted infrastructure provider for enterprise-grade entertainment solutions.

Global Scale
